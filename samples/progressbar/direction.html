Direction

(if select running,please change the value)

This sample demonstrates the wijprogressbar widget's value, labelAlign, orientation, and fillDirection options. To change the value, enter a number between 0 and 100 into the Value text box. To change the label alignment, click the Label Align drop-down arrow and select an alignment from the drop-down box. To set the fill direction, click the Fill Direction drop-down arrow and select a direction; east and west will give you a horizontal progress bar, and north and south will give you a vertical progress bar.
